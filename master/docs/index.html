    trovu.net – Web shortcuts: social, private, free.

    by Georg Jähnig & contributors

    Search 1000+ websites in a command-line way, with

    • curated and personal shortcuts,
    • organized by namespaces,
    • allowing multiple and typed arguments,
    • with maximum privacy.

    It's like DuckDuckGo bangs, but on steroids. And it's free.

    Use it from

    • your browser
    • smartphone, or
    • Raycast

    There is also a video summarizing Trovu in 30 seconds.

    Examples

    Web search

    • g berlin – Search Google for Berlin
    • w berlin – Go to the Wikipedia article about Berlin
    • fr.w berlin – Go to the French Wikipedia article about Berlin

    Travel

    • gm berlin – Open Google Maps with Berlin
    • gd london, liverpool – Search for a route on Google Directions from London to Liverpool
    • gfl ber, ibiza, fr, 28 – Search on Google Flights for a flight from Berlin to Ibiza, leaving next Friday, returning on the 28th
    • gfl1 ber, lpa, 1 - search one-way flights with Google from Berlin to Las Palmas, on the first of next month
    • bkg hh, 1, 3 - search accommodation on Booking.com in Hamburg from first to the third of next month
    • wg berlin – Search Wikipedia for all mentions of "berlin" via Google

    Video

    • yt cats – Search Youtube for cats
    • gv cats – Search Google Video for cats
    • tik cats – Search TikTok for cats
    • yt cats – Search Youtube for cats, sorted by date

    Programming

    • php strlen – Open PHP docs for strlen()
    • npm csv – Search the Node Package Manager for modules projects about CSV
